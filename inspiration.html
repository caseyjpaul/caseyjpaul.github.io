Site Inspiration

Kara Wags Photography
  • This is my friend Kara's photography website. I drew a lot of inspiration from her site when origianlly creating my own photography site. I liked how she showcased her work on her homepage as well as linking it to galleries and proof of her work. I also really liked how she had a "Fast Facts" section and a "Featured In" section.
